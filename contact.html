About

Thanks for your interest in Soundkiss Studio, Taipei Taiwan ROC!

This is a legacy page for a recording studio that I established in 2011, which I closed the door to for the final time in 2015.

The same day I caught a flight home to London, and so ended 8 years of life in Taiwan. In the rush to tie up all the loose ends, it was only when the plane was taking off did it fully sink in everyone and everything that I was leaving behind.

Years on and many more gigs, recordings and tours behind me, I still think about that other life; the studio sessions, friendships and the records we made over four years.

This page is my dedication to it.
