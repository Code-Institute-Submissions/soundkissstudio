About us


Thanks for your interest in Soundkiss Studio, Taipei Taiwan ROC!

This is a legacy web page for Soundkiss, a recording studio I established and ran in Taipei, Taiwan ROC from 2011-2015.

The same day we closed I had to rush to the airport to catch a flight to London, and so ended 8 years of life in Taiwan. Only when the plane was taking off did I fully realise everything I was leaving behind.

Years on and many more gigs, recordings and tours under my belt, I still remember that other life.

This page is my record.
